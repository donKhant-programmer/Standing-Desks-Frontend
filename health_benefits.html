Remove back pain

Investing in a quality adjustable standing desk is a great way to eliminate much of the pain you feel in your back as a result of sitting too much. This investment will also contribute to better overall posture while reducing hip pain, neck pain. And, you may even experience an uptick in productivity!

Improve Posture

Using a standing desk with proper ergonomics can help you have good posture at the computer. Your monitor should be at eye level, about 20 inches from your face at a 20-degree tilt, while you should bend your arms at 100-degree angles at your sides.
